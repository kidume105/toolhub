About QuickTools

QuickTools ni mkusanyiko wa tools za vitendo kwa creators, freelancers, na developers. Lengo letu ni kufanya kazi ndogo ndogo ziwe rahisi, zenye mvuto, na zipatikane hata kwenye hosting ya bure.

🎯 Mission

Kutoa tools ambazo zinatatua shida za kila siku: kupunguza video, kubadilisha audio, kupunguza image, na kusaidia workflows za writing na development.

⚙️ How it works

Tools nyingi zinafanya kazi kwenye browser moja kwa moja. Hii inasaidia privacy, simplicity, na kupunguza gharama za hosting mwanzoni.

🎨
Design first
UI imeboreshwa kuwa safi, yenye rangi zinazoonekana premium na button states zilizo wazi.
🔎
Search ready
Kuna browser metadata na sitemap ili pages ziwe tayari kwa indexing.
📈
Ready to monetize
Ukishaona traffic, unaweza kuongeza analytics, ads, affiliate links, au premium features.

Roadmap ya baadaye

Dashboard ya visitors, history ya conversions, dark/light theme toggle, na pages zaidi za SEO kwa kila tool.
